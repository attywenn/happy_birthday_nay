Happy 73rd Birthday, Nanay!

Greetings from Herrera fam!

7 decades of happiness

Born on December 10, 1952, in Santa Ana, San Mateo, Rizal, she was raised by Maxima Santos and Emilliano Esteban in Nangka, Marikina—a rural area that was then part of the Province of Rizal. According to her, her ancestry traces back to the Esteban and Santos familial clans.
She attended Nangka Elementary School in the 1960s but chose to leave school early to pursue dressmaking. In 2025, she celebrates her 73rd birthday.
